Homepage emission ratings

The ratings we show (F to A+) are based on those defined by Website Carbon and Sustainable Web Design. As the methodology for estimating web page emissions updates over time, the thresholds for these ratings also changes. That is because internet technologies can become more efficient over time and that lowers the impact of similarly sized pages. We show the ratings as they were at a given date.

Notes

We compiled Local Authorities and County Councils from the Local Government Association (England & Wales), COSLA (Scotland), and via manual searches for Northern Ireland. We have updated broken links. For Welsh council websites with a language-selection splash page, the Welsh language version of the home page has been chosen as the URL to test.

As of November 2024 some local authorities have started geo-blocking based on IP address. The result of this is that some of these local authorities are now inaccessible to our tool (because they are inaccessible to PageSpeed Insights). Their values will have to remain on the last real measurement.
